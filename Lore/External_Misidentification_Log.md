## **External Misidentification Log**

*How the World Explains What It Refuses to Understand*

---

### **Witness Crystals**

- **What they think it is:**  
  Corand: *Blasphemous false-memory engines*  
  Arderlands: *Propaganda lenses*  
  Beastriax: *Coward-records*

- **What it actually is:** Objective recording without interpretation.

- **Why they misidentify it:**  
  None of them believe truth can exist without intent. Neutral evidence breaks doctrine.

---

### **Resonance Rings**

- **What they think it is:**  
  Corand: *Soul-binding shackles*  
  Arderlands: *Loyalty collars*  
  Beastriax: *Weakness talismans*

- **What it actually is:** Bond stabilizers that only function through consent.

- **Why they misidentify it:**  
  Every existing bond system they know is coercive, so they assume that’s universal.

---

### **Stillwater Circlets**

- **Why they misidentify it:**  
  They can’t conceptualize instinct as something you *negotiate with*.

### **Pattern They Never Catch**

- If an artifact doesn’t respond to **authority**, they call it broken

- If it refuses **domination**, they call it cursed

- If it requires **consent**, they deny that function exists

- If it doesn’t escalate violence, they assume it’s incomplete

High Human artifacts don’t fail outside factions.

They **expose** them.

Which is worse.

# SPECIES SHEET — LYNARI

## Species Name

**Lynari**

Often mislabeled as:

- Beastfolk

- Feral hybrids

- Failed bloodlines  
  All incorrect. Conveniently incorrect.

---

## Classification

**High-Compatible Species (Lost Era)**  
Formerly recognized as a *High Species* before the purge of Sunfonesia and the erasure of stabilizer-dependent bloodlines.

---

## Physical Characteristics

- **Humanoid base physiology**

- **Feline traits**, varying by lineage:
  
  - Cat-like ears (highly expressive, involuntary tells)
  
  - Tail (primary balance and emotional indicator)
  
  - Fur patterns on arms, legs, ears, tail (often calico, tabby, or muted solid tones)

- Eyes adapted for:
  
  - Low-light vision
  
  - Motion tracking

- Height and build vary normally; no exaggerated “beast” proportions

**Important:**  
Lynari do not appear monstrous. Their appearance is *instinctive*, not ornamental. The world treats that as a flaw. It isn’t.

---

## Sensory & Neurological Traits

- Heightened spatial awareness

- Strong proprioception

- Reflexes faster than baseline humans

- Emotional states bleed subtly into posture, ears, and tail movement

These traits are frequently misread as lack of discipline.  
They are actually **unfiltered instinct**.

---

## Feral State

### Nature

The Lynari possess a **dual-state existence**:

- **Baseline State:** Fully sapient, controlled, rational

- **Feral State:** Instinct-forward, survival-optimized, emotionally amplified

This is not:

- Berserking

- Loss of self

- Madness

It is **a shift in priority**, not identity.

### Modern Instability

Without High Human anchoring:

- Feral transitions trigger too easily

- Recovery is harder

- Emotional overload can cascade into loss of control

This instability is why the world labels them “dangerous.”

The truth is simpler:  
They are **desynchronized**.

---

## Qi & Magic Affinity

### Baseline (Post-Purge Era)

- Qi flow: erratic, incomplete

- Magic expression: suppressed or volatile

- Training without anchoring yields diminishing returns

### True State (With High Human Bond)

- Qi flow stabilizes naturally

- Magic unlocks fully

- No artificial caps

- No feedback damage

- Feral state becomes *optional*, not compulsory

Lynari were never meant to function alone.

---

## Bond Compatibility

### True Bond Eligibility

Lynari are **one of the few species capable of true bond convergence** with High Humans.

Bond traits:

- Mutual resonance

- No domination

- No hierarchy

- Explicit consent required

Effects on Lynari:

- Instinct clarity

- Emotional grounding

- Full bloodline activation

- Restoration of lost traits

Kyle does not give Lynia strength.  
He removes what was blocking it.

## Psychological Profile

Common traits:

- Hypervigilance

- Strong survival instincts

- Difficulty trusting systems or authority

- Acute awareness of threat shifts

- Deep loyalty once trust is earned

These are trauma responses reinforced by centuries of systemic abuse, not inherent flaws.

Lynari fear is **data**, not weakness.

---

## Reproduction & Offspring (True Bond)

When bonded with a High Human:

Offspring traits:

- Hybrid Lynari–High Human

- Full Qi and magic access

- Sovereign Instinct present

- Feral state available without instability

- No locked traits

- No forced awakening thresholds

The child is not a “chosen one.”  
They are what the system was supposed to produce.

---

## Cultural Erasure

Pre-purge:

- Lynari were partners, not tools

- Bonds were chosen

- Instinct was respected

Post-purge:

- Lynari reduced to labor, soldiers, or breeding stock

- Forced bonds normalized

- Instinct punished

- History rewritten to frame them as unstable failures

This erasure is intentional.  
Their existence proves the lie.

**Lynia**

- Bloodline intact but locked

- Instinct sharp but punished

She is not special because she is unique.

## Core Thematic Function

The Lynari exist to prove one thing:

**The world is broken, not them.**

They are living evidence that:

- Control replaced balance

- Coercion replaced consent

- Stability was sacrificed for power

And once anchored again, they do not rebel.

They remember.
